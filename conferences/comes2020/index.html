H1

The more similar two given MPs are specialized, the more likely they cosponsor a motion.

H2

Members of parliament will more likely collaborate with MPs from the same state.

H3

Members of parliament will more likely collaborate with MPs in their districts' proximity.

H3A

Members of parliament elected by district vote will more likely collaborate with (any kind of) MPs.

H3B

Members of parliament elected by district vote will more likely collaborate with other district MPs.

H4 (Stability / Structural cooperation)

The more two given members of parliament co-signed / cospsonsored motions in the past, the more likely they will cosponsor a motion in the future.
